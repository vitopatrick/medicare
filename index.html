World-class Health Services

Our team approach to focusing on one patient at a time is the best in the world, not only because we invented it but also because we have been perfecting it for more than 150 years. Whether you require expertise to diagnose a persistent health concern or prompt answers to give you peace of mind, our team of private general practitioners are here for you.

Our GP appointments offer a patient-centered experience focused on your personal health concerns. With 30-minute or 60-minute appointments available, our experienced consultants have the time to fully explore your concerns through unhurried and comprehensive consultations.

We can schedule you quickly to resolve minor issues through your initial appointment. If you need further testing, our specialists will discuss your options, drawing on the resources of our full on-site diagnostic facility. Throughout your visit, you also will gain access to the expertise of over 7,300 Mayo Clinic specialists who are readily available to provide further support to our consultant team when needed. This means that no matter how rare or complex your condition is, you can have the confidence that our team of private consultants have the clinical expertise to support you.
